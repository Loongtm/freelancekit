Privacy Policy

FreelanceKit is a 100% client-side web app. We do not run a server and we do not store your documents remotely.

  • Local storage only: Your data stays in your browser unless you export files.
  • No tracking by default: No analytics or cookies unless explicitly added by you.
  • Imports/Exports: When you import or export JSON/CSV/PDF, files are processed locally in your browser.

For questions, contact: you@domain.com.
